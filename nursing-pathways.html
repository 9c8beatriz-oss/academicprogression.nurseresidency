⚠️ Important Reminders:
  • Tuition: Rates vary by residency status (in-state vs out-of-state) and delivery format (online vs on-campus). Always verify current rates on official program websites.
  • Credits & Length: Program length depends on entry point (BSN vs MSN), full-time vs part-time enrollment, and track requirements.
  • Clinical Hours: NP and DNP tracks require supervised clinical practice hours; placement is often student-coordinated with program approval.
  • Accreditation: Verify CCNE or ACEN accreditation for nursing programs; ALA accreditation for MLIS programs.
  • Funding: PhD programs often offer assistantships, fellowships, or tuition waivers. Check with individual programs.
  • Visa Restrictions: Some online programs may not meet F-1/J-1 visa requirements. International students should verify eligibility.
